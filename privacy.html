Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status. Cookies can be either "persistent" (stored on your device until they expire or you delete them) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function properly, including authentication and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the Site to improve performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver personalized advertising

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Site and your ability to access certain features.

You may also opt out of certain marketing cookies through third-party services such as the Network Advertising Initiative or Digital Advertising Alliance.

Third-Party Cookies
